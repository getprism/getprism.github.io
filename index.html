Prism

Access → Filter → Arrange → Play

A truly versatile music app for iOS

Prism is designed for those who prefer to keep their own music collection, but are dissatisfied with the inflexible nature of conventional music apps.

Quickly create interesting dynamic playlists using a handful of simple but powerful features, then play them with the tap of a button.

Access

Prism can access music already located on your device, or you can add a free Plex account to stream your music from anywhere in the world.

Plex is a media streaming application that runs on your computer. Prism takes advantage of its music streaming capabilities, but Plex is capable of video streaming and more.
